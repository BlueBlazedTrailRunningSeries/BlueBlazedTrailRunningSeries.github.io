Tracking Cookies

The Blue-Blazed Trail Running Series website ({{ site.url }}) (the website) does not make use of first-party cookies for any purpose. The website does not collect or store your browsing information while visiting the website.

Personal Information

The website posts race results, which may include the personal information of individual runners. This information is supplied to the Blue-Blazed Trail Running Series by the event organizers of events which the individuals have registered to participate in, and consists of information provided to the event organizers by the party who registered the entrant to the event. All information is posted under the reasonable expectation of availability of running race results and the personal information enclosed within.
